About Me

Solutions Engineer defined by a passion for problem solving with over five years of experience driving high-impact organizational initiatives across the United States and India. My professional trajectory combines roles at Fortune 500 leaders including Schneider Electric and Tata Consultancy Services with strategic engagements at the GIFT City Public-Private Partnership. In my latest role within Schneider Electric's AI Hub, our team delivered a one-of-its-kind proof-of-concept AI-based fault location system for a major US power utility modeled to significantly reduce operator and field labor hours. Leveraging such experiences to deliver secure, scalable solutions for diverse sectors including data centers, power and grid, life sciences, and public utilities, I am uniquely adept at blending deep technical expertise with business strategy. This enables me to architect enterprise-grade AI and analytics ecosystems explicitly engineered to meet critical business KPIs such as operational efficiency, cost reduction, and yield optimization.

Academic foundation rooted in an M.S. from the NYU Tandon School of Engineering and NYU Stern School of Business where I was recognized as a Merit Tuition Fee Scholar. My technical authority is sharpened through specialized coursework, credentials, and workshops associated with Stanford University, IIT Bombay, SRM Institute of Science & Technology, National Forensic Sciences University, and the Government of India's premier R&D institute, C-DAC. This expertise is further distinguished by my tenure supporting state-level public agencies where I implemented digital transformation strategies to accelerate critical safety operations. I am a published author recognized by the ISSN National Centre for India holding a Letter of Appreciation from the President of the Association of Indian Universities. Additionally, I am an inventor with a US Patent pending and an IEEE paper submission.

In my free time, I run PeakWhale™, a Boston-based open source initiative building enterprise-grade solutions that are open by default. I also channel my energy into traveling to new places, always eager to meet new people and learn from fresh perspectives.
